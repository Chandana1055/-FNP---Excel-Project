# -FNP---Excel-Project
FNP Sales Analysis Dashboard 📊  This project presents an interactive sales analysis dashboard built using Microsoft Excel. It analyzes key metrics such as total orders, revenue, customer spending, and delivery performance.This project helps in identifying sales trends, customer behavior, and business growth opportunities.
Key Highlights
The business processed a total of 1,000 orders, generating a revenue of ₹35,20,984, indicating strong overall sales performance.
The average customer spend is approximately ₹3,520.98, reflecting a healthy customer purchase value.
The average order delivery time is 5.53 days, suggesting efficient logistics with potential scope for optimization.
Sales Performance Insights
Revenue by Occasion shows peak sales during events like anniversaries and festivals, indicating strong seasonal demand patterns.
Revenue by Category highlights top-performing product categories such as cakes, soft toys, and gift items, helping identify profitable segments.
Top 5 Products by Revenue reveal key contributors to overall income, enabling focused inventory and marketing strategies.
Time-Based Trends
Revenue by Months demonstrates fluctuations, with certain months generating significantly higher revenue, suggesting seasonal trends and promotional opportunities.
Revenue by Hour (Order Time) provides insights into customer purchasing behavior throughout the day, helping optimize marketing campaigns and staffing.
Geographical Insights
Top 10 Cities by Orders identifies major markets driving sales volume, allowing the business to target high-performing locations and expand strategically.
Interactive Features
The dashboard includes filters (slicers) for:
Order Date (Year/Month)
Delivery Time
Occasion
These allow users to dynamically explore data and gain customized insights based on specific criteria.
